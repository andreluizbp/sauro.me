About

Hi, my name is Andersson Quegi, but you can call me Sauro!
I'm a brazilian ux designer, front-end engineer and planning strategist currently living in Curitiba, Brasil.

Since 2005 working professionally with web and mobile projects coordination. From concept passing by the design until to content development, always focusing in the user experience, SEO and performance. I really enjoy working on perfecting strategies to build a beautiful, valuable and simple content.

I spent over 10 years living as a freelancer, I studied a lot about design, marketing and development. Over the years I acquired a lot of knowledge in this area, was not easy and went through very bad time, but together it, was born my passion and my decision: that's what I want to do for life and I really love what I do. :)

I'm a strategist, always keep myself updated with the latest web trends and technology. If I don't know how to do, I know how to find the solution.

When I'm not working, I like to walk with my dogs, clean the house, cycling and playing football. You can learn more from me on Twitter, see some of my photos in Instagram, texts recommend in the Medium and professional details on LinkedIn.

If you have some really cool project that I would like to present or work together, let's talk!
